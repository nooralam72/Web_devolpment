Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Born: October 15, 1931

Died: July 27, 2015

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He is known as the 'Missile Man of India' for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology.
